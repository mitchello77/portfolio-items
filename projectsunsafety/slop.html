Slop on Sunscreen

Sunscreen is one of the most common methods of sun protection. SunSmart recommends SPF 30 or higher broad spectrum, water resistant sunscreen. No sunscreen provides full protection so never rely on sunscreen alone for sun protection. During the daily sun protection times (when the UV Index is 3 and above), combine sunscreen with sun-protective clothing, a broad-brimmed hat that protects the face, head, neck and ears, shade and sunglasses.It is iportant to apply sunscreen 20 minutes before you go outside and again every two hours (whether or not the label tells you to do this). Finally, make sre you apply generous amounts of sunscreen. It needs to thinly cover all skin so it can properly create a shield.
