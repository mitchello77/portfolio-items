Self Examination

How to check

All Australians should become familiar with their skin. Check all of your skin, not just sun-exposed areas. If you notice anything unusual, including any change in shape, colour or size of a spot, or the development of a new spot, visit your doctor as soon as possible. Confusion is often sort over moles or melanomas. Most of us have moles and devolope randomly. As a general rule of thumb, if it looks odd or is itchy then have it check by a doctor.

The process

Many melanomas r other forms of cancers are discovered by people themselves or by a family member.
It's time to go through a do-it-yourself skin examination: first find a room with good light and a full length mirror. If you are on your own have a hand-held mirror to check difficult to see areas. Undress completely and start examining your skin, body part-by-body part, until you have checked your whole body.
