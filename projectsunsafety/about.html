About Project: SunSafety

Who are we?

We are a group of people with a common dream; increase the awareness of our beautiful yet destructive sun, nationally. It is that simple. Founded in 2015, we are new players in the field but we believe that there can never be too many when spreading a cause. Working hand in hand with other organisations such as SunSmart™ and the various Cancer Council’s, not as a competitor, but as an ally to achieve our joint goal; more awareness, no cancer and more fun!

Aims and Objectives

The aims of Project: SunSafety are to increase awareness about the dangers of excessive sun exposure, minimise the human cost of skin cancer and explore the how and why behind.

We achieve this by the following objectives:

  • Sharing, assisting in and funding research that explores cancer and preventions

  • Rally for supportive environments and infrastructure addressing UV over-exposure

  • Providing and building a community of support for all but especially the younger generation

  • Creating a knowledge base of factual and relevant information
